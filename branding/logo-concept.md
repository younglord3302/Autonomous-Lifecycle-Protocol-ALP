# ALP Branding Concept

## Name
**ALP** - Autonomous Lifecycle Protocol

## Tagline
"Markdown is for humans. ALP is for machines."
"The first open standard for autonomous software engineering."

## Logo Concept
- A stylized, geometric "A" that looks like a continuous loop (representing the Loop Engine).
- A node-and-edge graph forming the letters A-L-P (representing the Dependency Graph).
- Clean, monospaced typography for the wordmark to emphasize its machine-readable nature.
- Colors: Terminal Green (#00FF00) or Electric Blue (#00A3FF) on a very dark charcoal background (#121212).

## Voice & Tone
- Precise, deterministic, and unambiguous.
- Developer-centric but looking forward to the AI-native future.
- Pragmatic (focusing on actual execution and verification, not just abstract planning).
